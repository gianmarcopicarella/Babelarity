Ishaq ibn Ali (died April 1147) was the eighth and last Almoravid king who reigned shortly in 1147.
Ibrahim was the uncle of his predecessor as emir, Ibrahim ibn Tashfin, and was killed after the conquest of Marrakech by the Almohads.
