Unión La Calera is a Chilean football club based in La Calera, that is a current member of the Campeonato Nacional. Their main local rivals are San Luis de Quillota.
The club was founded on January 26, 1954 following the merger of five local clubs of La Calera: Tifón, Minas Navío, Cóndor, Cemento Melón and Calera Comercio.
