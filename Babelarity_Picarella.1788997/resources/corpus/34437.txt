Hypericaceae is a plant family in the order Malpighiales, comprising ca 6 genera and 590 species. Molecular data supports its monophyly. Some systematists treat it as a subfamily of the Clusiaceae. When it is accepted as a distinct family, it contains the following genera:
Cratoxylum Blume
Eliea Cambess.
Harungana Lamarck
Hypericum L.
Lianthus N. Robson
Psorospermum Spach
Santomasia N. Robson
Thornea Breedlove & McClintock
Triadenum Rafinesque
Vismia Vand.
