Tatsuhiko Kubo (久保 竜彦, Kubo Tatsuhiko, born June 18, 1976 in Chikuzen, Fukuoka) is a former Japanese football player who was capped 32 times and scored 11 goals for the Japanese national team.
He retired in 2012.
