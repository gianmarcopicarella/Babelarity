Eduardo Astengo (15 August 1905, in Lima, Perú – 3 December 1969) was a Perùvian football Midfielder.
During his career he has made one appearances for the Peruvian national team at the Fifa World Cup 1930 against. His career in club football was spent in Universitario de Deportes.
