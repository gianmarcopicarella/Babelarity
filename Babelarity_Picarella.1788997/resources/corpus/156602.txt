Honkajoki is a municipality of Finland.
It is located in the province of Western Finland and is part of the Satakunta region. The population of Honkajoki is 1,795 (31 March 2016) and the municipality covers an area of 331.22 km2 (127.88 sq mi) of which 1.80 km2 (0.69 sq mi) is inland water (1 January 2011). The population density is 5.42/km2 (14.0/sq mi).
The municipality is unilingually Finnish.
