Stanislaus "Tau" Kobierski (15 November 1910 in Düsseldorf — 18 November 1972) was a German football (soccer) player.
Between 1931 and 1941, he played 26 times and scored 9 goals for the Germany national football team. He participated in the 1934 FIFA World Cup, and scored Germany's first ever World Cup goal, in the first round 5-2 win over Belgium. His home team was Fortuna Düsseldorf.
