Jennifer Carole "Jen" Ledger (born 8 December 1989) is an English drummer and co-vocalist for the American Christian rock/hard rock band Skillet. At the age of eighteen she became Skillet's drummer, when their former drummer Lori Peters retired.
Ledger relocated to the United States at age 16 to major in drums at Living Light School of Worship in Kenosha, Wisconsin.
She has toured as Skillet's drummer since the "Comatose Tour", and has also provided vocals on the albums Awake and Rise.
