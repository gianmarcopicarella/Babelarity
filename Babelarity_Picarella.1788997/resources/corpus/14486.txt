Marat Rifkatovich Bikmaev (born 1 January 1986 in Tashkent) is an Uzbekistani football striker of Volga Tatar origin who plays for Lokomotiv Tashkent FK in the Uzbek League.
On 20 June 2012 FC Aktobe announced the signing of Bikmaev. He scored first goal for Aktobe in first official match on 20 June 2012 in Kazakhstan Cup match against FC Lashyn.
He is a member of the national team, and has played 28 matches and scored 2 goals (as of 10 June 2012) since his debut in 2004.
