Daverdisse is a Walloon municipality of Belgium located in the province of Luxembourg. On 1 January 2007 the municipality, which covers 56.4 km², had 1,372 inhabitants, giving a population density of 24.3 inhabitants per km².
The municipality consists of the following sub-municipalities: Daverdisse proper, Gembes, Haut-Fays, and Porcheresse.
