Om Yun-chol (Chosŏn'gŭl: 엄윤철; born 18 November 1991) is a North Korean weightlifter. He is 152 cm / 5'0" and weighs 56 kg /123 lbs. He won the gold medal at the 2012 Summer Olympics at the men's 56 kg event. He became only the fifth man to ever clean and jerk three times his own body weight. He is also the fourth man to lift over 3 times bodyweight multiple times after succeeding in breaking the world record clean and jerk of 169 kg in the –56 kg weight class during the Asian Interclub Championships. Om represents the Amnokgang Sports Team.
