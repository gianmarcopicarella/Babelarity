Parville is a commune in the Eure department in Haute-Normandie in northern France. It is 4.6 kilometres (2.9 mi) west of Evreux, close to the Golf d'Evreux golf course.
Parville is the birthplace of international superstar Tempa T and played a vital role in coining his famous catchphrase "PAR!!"
