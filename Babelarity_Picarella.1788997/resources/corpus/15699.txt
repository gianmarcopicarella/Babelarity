The Catalan Cycling Week (Setmana Catalana de Ciclisme in Catalan) was a multi-stage road bicycle race held in Catalonia, Spain. It was last organised in 2005 as a 2.HC race on the UCI Europe Tour. Setmana Catalana was cancelled in 2006 and it is no longer part of the UCI Calendar.
The first winner of the race was José Pérez-Francés in 1963.
