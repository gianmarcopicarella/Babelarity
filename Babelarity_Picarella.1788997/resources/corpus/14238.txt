The Men's parallel bars competition at the 2012 Summer Olympics in London, United Kingdom. The event was held at the North Greenwich Arena on 7 August.
The top eight qualifiers in the qualification phase (limit two per NOC) advanced to the apparatus final. Qualification scores were then ignored, with only final round scores counting.
All times are British Summer Time (UTC+1)
* Only 2 athletes per country can reach the final.
