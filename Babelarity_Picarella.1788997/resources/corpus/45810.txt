Shades of God is the third album by Paradise Lost. It features the use of growling noticeable in the band's previous efforts although shows the beginning of the transition to more 'clean' vocals seen on the album 'Icon'.
All songs written by Nick Holmes and Greg Mackintosh.
Nick Holmes - vocals
Matthew Archer - drums
Stephen Edmondson - bass
Aaron Aedy - guitars
Gregor Mackintosh - guitars
Sarah Marrion - vocals
Robert John Godfrey - keyboards
George Chin - photography
Dave McKean - cover art, design, illustrations
Simon Efemey - engineering, producer, mixing
