Tiger parrots are members of the genus Psittacella in the Psittaculidae family getting its name through having tiger-striped backs. Established by Hermann Schlegel in 1871, the genus contains the following species, all of which are endemic to the island of New Guinea.
Madarasz's tiger parrot (Psittacella madaraszi)
Modest tiger parrot (Psittacella modesta)
Painted tiger parrot (Psittacella picta)
Brehm's tiger parrot (Psittacella brehmii)
