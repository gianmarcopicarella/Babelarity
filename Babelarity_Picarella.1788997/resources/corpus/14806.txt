Hitoshi Sogahata (曽ヶ端準, Sogahata Hitoshi, born August 2, 1979) is a Japanese football player who has been playing for Kashima Antlers for fifteen years straight, which amount to his entire career so far.
The goalkeeper was part of the Japanese 2004 Olympic football team, who exited in the first round, having finished fourth in group B, below group winners Paraguay, Italy and Ghana. He is a product of the Antlers youth system.
