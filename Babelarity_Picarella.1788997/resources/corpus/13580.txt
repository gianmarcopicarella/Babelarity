Masato Morishige (森重 真人, Morishige Masato, born 21 May 1987 in Asakita-ku, Hiroshima, Japan) is a Japanese football player who plays for Japanese club FC Tokyo.
Morishige competed for the Japan national football team at the 2008 Summer Olympics.
