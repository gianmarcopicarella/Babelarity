John Javier Restrepo (born August 22, 1977 in Medellín, Colombia) is football midfielder who played for the Colombia national football team between 2001 and 2010. He currently plays for Celaya.
He returned to play for his old club Deportivo Independiente Medellín, after leaving Tigres in Mexico. His past clubs include Independiente Medellín (Colombia), Atlético Bucaramanga, and Cruz Azul (Mexico).
