Natalia Sergeyevna Ishchenko (Russian: Наталья Серге́евна Ищенко) (born 8 April 1986) is a Russian competitor in synchronized swimming and a triple Olympic gold medalist.
Natalia was a member of the Russian gold medal team in 2008 and 2012. She also won gold in the women's duet at the 2012 Summer Olympics with Svetlana Romashina.
Natalia took a break from the sport in 2013 after giving birth to her son. She returned in 2015, winning both the women's duet and team events at the European Synchro Cup in Haarlemmermeer, qualifying for the 2016 Olympic Games in Rio de Janeiro. She has announced her plan to retire on 1 January 2017.
