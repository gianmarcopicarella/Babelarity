Ikue Ōtani (大谷 育江, Ōtani Ikue, born August 18, 1965) is a Japanese voice actress from Kashiwazaki, Niigata, affiliated with Mausu Promotion. She is best known for her roles in the Pokémon series (as Pikachu), One Piece (as Tony Tony Chopper), Naruto (as Konohamaru Sarutobi), Detective Conan (as Mitsuhiko Tsuburaya), and Uchi no Sanshimai (as Fu). She is currently attached to Mausu Promotion. Her pet name is "Iku-chan". She is known for playing both male and female roles, and sometimes plays multiple roles in one production. She is a native of Tokyo, but grew up in Niigata Prefecture.
In January 2006, Ōtani took a temporary leave of absence from her work due to her pregnancy. Her ongoing roles were replaced with other voice actors.
