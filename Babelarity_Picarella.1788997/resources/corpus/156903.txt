Herent (Dutch pronunciation: [ˈɦeːrənt]) is a municipality located in the Belgian province of Flemish Brabant. The municipality comprises the towns of Herent proper, Veltem-Beisem and Winksele. On January 1, 2010, Herent had a total population of 20,030. The total area is 32.73 km² which gives a population density of 610 inhabitants per km². The current mayor (as of 2013) is Marleen Schouteden.
