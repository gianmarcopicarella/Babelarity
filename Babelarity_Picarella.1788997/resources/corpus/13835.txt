Adrien Théaux (born 18 September 1984) is a French World Cup alpine ski racer and non-commissioned officer. He made his World Cup debut in February 2004 at age 19.
Théaux represented France at two Winter Olympics and five World Championships; his best finish is a bronze medal in the Super-G in 2015 at Beaver Creek, Colorado.
Through January 2016, he has three World Cup victories and thirteen podiums.
