Lu Chunlong (simplified Chinese: 陆春龙; traditional Chinese: 陸春龍; pinyin: Lù Chūnlóng; born April 8, 1989 in Jiangyin, Jiangsu) is a male Chinese trampoline gymnast who competed in the 2008 Summer Olympics where he won the Gold medal. Four years later, at the 2012 London Olympics, Lu got the bronze medal while Dong Dong, the bronze medalist from Beijing, won the competition.
