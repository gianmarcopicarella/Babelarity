Raducz [ˈradut͡ʂ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nowy Kawęczyn, within Skierniewice County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies on the Rawka River, approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south-east of Nowy Kawęczyn, 16 km (10 mi) south-east of Skierniewice, and 59 km (37 mi) east of the regional capital Łódź. It was probably founded in the 18th century.
In 2004 the village had a population of 30.
