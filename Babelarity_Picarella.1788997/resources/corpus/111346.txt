The arrondissement of Orléans is an arrondissement of France in the Loiret department in the Centre-Val de Loire region. It has 122 communes.
The cantons of the arrondissement of Orléans are:
Beaugency
Châteauneuf-sur-Loire
La Ferté-Saint-Aubin
Fleury-les-Aubrais
Lorris (partly)
Meung-sur-Loire
Olivet
Orléans-1
Orléans-2
Orléans-3
Orléans-4
Pithiviers (partly)
Saint-Jean-de-Braye
Saint-Jean-de-la-Ruelle
Saint-Jean-le-Blanc
Sully-sur-Loire (partly)
