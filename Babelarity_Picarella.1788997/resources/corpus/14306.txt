The 2007 Torneo Godó was a men's professional tennis tournament that was part of the International Series Gold of the 2007 ATP Tour. It was the 55th edition of the Torneo Godó and took place from April 23–29, 2007 in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. Rafael Nadal won the singles title.
 Rafael Nadal defeated  Guillermo Cañas 6–3, 6–4
