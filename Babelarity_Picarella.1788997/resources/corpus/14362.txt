Sven Schultze (born July 11, 1978) is a retired German professional basketball player. Schultze played as power forward or center. He is 2.06 m (6'9") in height and he weighs 110 kg (242 pounds). His last team was Eisbären Bremerhaven of the German League, in the 2014–15 season.
Alba Berlin
Basketball Bundesliga (4): 1998–99, 1999–00, 2000–01, 2001–02
BBL-Pokal (4): 1999, 2002, 2013, 2014
From 2000 to 2011 Schultze was a member of the German national basketball team and played 121 games with them.
