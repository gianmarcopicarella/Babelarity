The Fédération Malagasy de Football (FMF) is the governing body of football in Madagascar. It was founded in 1961, affiliated to FIFA in 1964 and to CAF in 1963. It organizes the national football league and the national team.
On March 19, 2008, the FMF was suspended by FIFA.[1] On May 19, 2008, the suspension was lifted.[2]
