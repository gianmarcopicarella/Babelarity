Grajal de Campos (Spanish pronunciation: [ɡɾaˈxal de ˈkampos]), Grayal de Campos in Leonese language, is a municipality located in the province of León, Castile and León, Spain. According to the 2010 census (INE), the municipality has a population of 246 inhabitants. There is a historical castle in the town.
