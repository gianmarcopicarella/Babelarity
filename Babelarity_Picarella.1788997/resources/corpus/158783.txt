Santo Antônio do Jardim (Portuguese meaning "Saint Anthony of the garden") is a municipality in the eastern part of the state of São Paulo in Brazil. The population is 6,053 (2015 est.) in an area of 109.96 km². The elevation is 850 m. Santo Antônio do Jardim is located north of the state capital named São Paulo and eastnortheast of Campinas. The state of Minas Gerais is bounded to the east.
