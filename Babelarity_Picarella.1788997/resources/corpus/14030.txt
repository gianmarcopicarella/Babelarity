Taner Yalçın (Turkish pronunciation: [ˈt̪anæɾ jaɫˈt͡ʃɯn], born 18 February 1990) is a Turkish footballer who plays for Kayseri Erciyesspor.
He made his debut for the main team on 24 August 2008 in a game against Eintracht Frankfurt. He has played a game for the German U-19 national team.
On 11 September 2011, Sunday, at 1st week of 2011-12 Süper Lig, Yalçın was subbed on Doka Madureira and made his official Süper Lig debut, against Galatasaray, in which ended 2–0.
