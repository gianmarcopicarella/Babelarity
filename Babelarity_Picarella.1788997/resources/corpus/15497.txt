The 2011 If Stockholm Open was a professional tennis tournament played on indoor hard courts. It was the 43rd edition of the tournament, which was part of the 2011 ATP World Tour. It took place in Stockholm, Sweden between 17 and 23 October 2011.
 Gaël Monfils defeated  Jarkko Nieminen, 7–5, 3–6, 6–2 
It was Monfils' 1st title of the year and 4th of his career.
 Rohan Bopanna /  Aisam-ul-Haq Qureshi defeated  Marcelo Melo /  Bruno Soares, 6–1, 6–3
