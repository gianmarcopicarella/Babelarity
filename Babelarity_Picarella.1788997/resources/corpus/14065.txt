The 2010 Open de Moselle was a tennis tournament played on indoor hard courts. It was the 13th edition of the Open de Moselle, and is part of the ATP World Tour 250 Series of the 2010 ATP World Tour. It was held at the Arènes de Metz in Metz, France, from 20 - 26 September 2010.
Seeds are based on the rankings of 13 September 2010.
