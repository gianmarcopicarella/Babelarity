Princess Josephine Friederike Luise of Baden (21 October 1813 – 19 June 1900) was born at Mannheim, the daughter of Charles, Grand Duke of Baden and his wife, Stéphanie de Beauharnais. Through her eldest son Leopold and also through her daughter Marie, she is the ancestress of the Romanian royal family and the Yugoslav Royal Family. Through her daughter Marie, she is also the ancestress of the Belgian royal family and the Grand Ducal family of Luxemburg.
On 21 October 1834 at Karlsruhe, she married Karl Anton Joachim Zephyrinus Friedrich Meinrad, Prince of Hohenzollern-Sigmaringen, son of Charles, Prince of Hohenzollern-Sigmaringen (1785–1853) and his wife Marie Antoinette Murat (1793–1847).
Their children were:
Leopold (1835–1905).
Stephanie (1837–1859), Queen consort of Pedro V, King of Portugal.
Carol I (1839–1914), King of Romania.
Anton (1841–1866), mortally wounded at Königgrätz.
Friedrich (1843–1904).
Marie (1845–1912), married Prince Philippe, Count of Flanders.
She died at Sigmaringen in 1900.
