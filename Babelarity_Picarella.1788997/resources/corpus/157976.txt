Mamanguaipe is a municipality in the state of Paraíba in the Northeast Region of Brazil.
The municipality contains part of the 4,052 hectares (10,010 acres) Guaribas Biological Reserve, a fully protected conservation unit created in 1990. It also contains the Pau-Brasil Ecological Station, which protects a stand of endangered Pau-Brazil (Caesalpinia echinata) trees.
