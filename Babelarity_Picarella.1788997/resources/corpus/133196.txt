The Roman Catholic Diocese of Lai (Latin: Laien(sis)) is a diocese in Lai in the Ecclesiastical province of N'Djamena in Chad.
November 28, 1998: Established as Diocese of Lai from the Diocese of Doba and Diocese of Moundou
The Pro-Cathedral is the Cathédrale de la Sainte-Famille in Lai.
Bishops of Lai (Roman rite)
Bishop Miguel Angel Sebastián Martínez, M.C.C.I. (since November 28, 1998)
