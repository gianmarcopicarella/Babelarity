Monolith Productions (or simply Monolith) is a Kirkland, Washington-based computer game developer known for their games Blood, the No One Lives Forever series, F.E.A.R and more recently, Middle-earth: Shadow of Mordor. Between 1997 and 1999, Monolith also published games, some developed by the studio, some by third parties.
In 2004, Monolith Productions was acquired by Warner Bros., a subsidiary of Time Warner.
