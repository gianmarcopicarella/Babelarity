Kristoffer Jon Polaha is an American film and television actor best known for his roles as Jason Matthews in North Shore, Nathaniel "Baze" Bazile in Life Unexpected, and Henry Butler in Ringer.
Polaha starred on ABC's mid-season replacement series, Miss Guided, and appeared in the Fox Network television series Tru Calling. He next played Nate Bazile, a bar owner who discovers he has a teenage daughter, on the series Life Unexpected, during its two season run. He played Henry Butler on the CW series Ringer until the series cancelation in 2012. Polaha starred as John Galt in Atlas Shrugged: Part III, which was released September 12, 2014.
