Lichtspielhaus (obsolete German for "cinema"; literally "light-play house") is the German NDH-metal band Rammstein's second DVD, and was released on December 1, 2003. It is a compilation of all their videos to date and some live performances, commercial advertisements, trailers and "makings-of".
