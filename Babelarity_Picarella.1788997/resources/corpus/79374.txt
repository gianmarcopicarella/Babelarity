Marissa Madeline Carroll (born March 18, 1996) is an American actress. She is well known for starring with Kevin Costner in Swing Vote (2008).
Carroll was born and raised in Los Angeles. Her mother is a homemaker and her father is a contractor. She has three brothers: Ned, Jack and Dylan.
She began modeling when she was 3 years old. At the age of four, she was discovered by her theatrical agent 'Wendy' in a nail shop in Sherman Oaks..
She appeared in a variety of television shows, including NCIS, Cold Case, Grey's Anatomy, Lie to Me, Night Stalker, Wanted and Lost. She also starred in films, such as The Santa Clause 3: The Escape Clause, Resident Evil: Extinction, and for her prominent roles for Swing Vote and The Spy Next Door. She played Janie in Mr. Popper's Penguins. In 2010, she starred in the film adaptation of Wendelin Van Draanen's novel, Flipped. She also appeared in commercials, such as Subway, Allstate, Avery NoteTabs, Mr. Clean and Camp Rock Sweepstakes Back-to-School.
On February 14, 2013, it was announced that Carroll will star in The CW's drama Blink.
