Rockstar London, Ltd. is a British video game developer, and in 2005 was the second newest studio to join the Rockstar Games family. Notable productions include Manhunt 2 for the PlayStation 2. Rockstar London is located on Kings Road, London, in the same building as Rockstar International, a publishing headquarters for the UK, Europe, Asia, Japan, Africa, Australia and South America.
In June 2008, Rockstar London were nominated for the Develop Industry Excellence Awards 2008 in the category of Best New UK/European Studio alongside Finblade, Konami Paris, Oxygen Studios and Doublesix.
