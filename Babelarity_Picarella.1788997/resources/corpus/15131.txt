Li Xuerui (born January 24, 1991 in Chongqing) is a female badminton player from China and one of the most successful players of her time. As of March 2016 she is the World No 3 women's singles player and the reigning Olympic Champion. She graduate BA from Huachiao University and runner up in the 2013 and 2014 World Championships Competition.
