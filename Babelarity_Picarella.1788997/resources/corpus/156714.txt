Koga (古賀市, Koga-shi) is a city located in Fukuoka Prefecture, Japan. It is north east of Fukuoka City. The city was founded on October 1, 1997.
As of 2009, the city has an estimated population of 58,250 and a population density of about 1,383 persons per km². The total area is 42.11 km².
Koga Station is on the Kagoshima main line.
