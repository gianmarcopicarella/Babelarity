Juuka (Swedish: Juga) is a municipality of Finland.
It is located in the province of Eastern Finland and is part of the North Karelia region. The municipality has a population of 5,022 (31 March 2016) and covers an area of 1,846.57 square kilometres (712.96 sq mi) of which 344.78 km2 (133.12 sq mi) is water. The population density is 3.34 inhabitants per square kilometre (8.7/sq mi).
The municipality is unilingually Finnish.
