'Coronel Pringles is a department of San Luis Province, Argentina.
With an area of 4,484 km2 (1,731 sq mi) it borders to the north with the departments of Ayacucho and San Martín, to the east with Chacabuco and General Pedernera, to the south with Pedernera, La Capital, and Belgrano.
