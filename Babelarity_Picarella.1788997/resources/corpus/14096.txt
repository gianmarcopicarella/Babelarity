Evgeni Petrov (born 25 May 1978 in Ufa) is a Russian professional road bicycle racer, who currently rides for UCI ProTeam Tinkoff. He won the 11th stage of the 2010 Giro d'Italia, Lucera to L'Aquila. He was ejected from the 2005 Tour de France and suspended from cycling for two weeks after his haemetocrit was deemed over the limit by morning controls on the tenth stage.
