The 1979 South African Grand Prix was a Formula One motor race held on 3 March 1979 at Kyalami.
Lap Leaders: Jean-Pierre Jabouille 1 lap (1); Gilles Villeneuve 39 laps (2-14, 53-78); Jody Scheckter 38 laps (15-52).
Last race James Hunt would finish.
Note: Only the top five positions are included for both sets of standings.
