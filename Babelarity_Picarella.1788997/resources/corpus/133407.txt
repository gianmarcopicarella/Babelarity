The Roman Catholic Diocese of Tubarão (Latin: Dioecesis Tubaraoënsis) is a diocese located in the city of Tubarão in the Ecclesiastical province of Florianópolis in Brazil.
December 28, 1954: Established as Diocese of Tubarão from the Metropolitan Archdiocese of Florianópolis
Bishops of Tubarão (Roman rite)
Anselmo Pietrulla, O.F.M. (1955–1981)
Osório Bebber, O.F.M. Cap. (17 September 1981–1992)
Hilário Moser, S.D.B. (1992–2004)
Jacinto Bergmann (2004–2009)
Wilson Tadeu Jönck, S.C.I. (18 July 2010–2011)
João Francisco Salm (24 November 2012–present)
