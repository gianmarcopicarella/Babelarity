Darya Kustova (Belarusian: Дар'я Кустава; Russian: Дарья Кустова; born 29 May 1986) is a former professional Belarusian tennis player.
Her highest WTA singles ranking is 117, which she reached on January 18, 2010. Her career high in doubles was 66, set on July 7, 2008.
Kustova won one WTA doubles titles Partnering Mariya Koryttseva, 7 singles and 29 doubles ITF titles.
Playing for Belarus at the Fed Cup, Kustova has a win–loss of 11–4.
Darya Kustova retired from tennis before the 2013 season.
