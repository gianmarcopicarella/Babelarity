Santpedor (Catalan pronunciation: [ˌsampəˈðɔ]) is a municipality and town in the comarca of Bages, central Catalonia, Spain. It is located north to Manresa, in the flattest sector of Bages. Sights include the Romanesque-Gothic church of St. Peter, and the Hermitage of St. Francis. Notable natives of Santpedor include Josep Guardiola, former football player and manager of Manchester City.
