Kazumichi Takagi (高木 和道, Takagi Kazumichi, born 21 November 1980 in Yasu, Shiga, Japan) is a Japanese football defender currently playing for J1 team Júbilo Iwata.
After quitting Kyoto Sangyo University in Kyoto in 2000, Takagi signed for S-Pulse where he has played since, save for a brief loan spell with Vissel Kobe. He made his international debut for Japan on August 20, 2008 in a friendly against Uruguay at Sapporo Dome.
