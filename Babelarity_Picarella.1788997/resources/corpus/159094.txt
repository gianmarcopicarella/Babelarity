Briviesca is a municipality and city located in the province of Burgos, Castile and León, Spain. According to the 2007 census (INE), the municipality has a population of 7,227 inhabitants. In 2015, a census was done that recorded 7,129 residents.
The municipality of Briviesca is made up of five towns: Briviesca (seat or capital), Cameno, Quintanillabón, Revillagodos and Valdazo.
