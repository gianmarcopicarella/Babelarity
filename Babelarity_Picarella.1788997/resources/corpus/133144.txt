The Diocese of Kaposvár (Latin: Dioecesis Kaposvarensis) is a diocese located in the city of Kaposvár in the Ecclesiastical province of Veszprém in Hungary.
May 31, 1993: Established as Diocese of Kaposvár from the Diocese of Veszprém
Bishops of Kaposvár(Roman rite)
