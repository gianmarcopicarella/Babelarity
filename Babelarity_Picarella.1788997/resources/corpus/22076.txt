Anabel Torres Restrepo (born 28 December 1948) is a Colombian poet and translator.
She studied Modern Languages at the University of Antioquia in Medellín and made a master at the Institute of Social Studies in The Hague. She was assistant director at the National Library of Colombia (Biblioteca Nacional de Colombia).
