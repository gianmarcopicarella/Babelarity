Laurent Bonnart (born 25 December 1979 in Chambray-lès-Tours) is a French football player. He currently plays for Châteauroux.
Bonnart played with Le Mans UC72, with whom he began his career. He was a regular in the team from the start of the 2001–02 season, having made his debut in Ligue 2 in the season's first match.
In 2007, he moved from Le Mans to Marseille.
In 2010, he moved to Monaco on a free transfer.
